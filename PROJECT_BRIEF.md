# iLab® AI Education Platform
## Business Summary & Marketing Strategy Document

---

## Executive Summary

iLab® is a premium AI education platform designed to empower children and teenagers (ages 8-18) with cutting-edge artificial intelligence and coding skills. Through an intensive 8-week transformation journey, students learn to launch real products, solve real problems, and lead real change in the AI-powered future.

**Mission**: To give children the ultimate advantage in tomorrow's world by providing world-class AI education that transforms curious learners into confident creators.

**Vision**: To become the leading AI education platform that prepares the next generation of innovators, entrepreneurs, and technology leaders.

## Design & Brand Identity

### Visual Design System: Neubrutalism Aesthetics

**Color Palette**:
- **Primary**: Warm Coral (HSL: 14, 90%, 65%)
- **Accents**: Purple, Yellow, Blue, Green (high saturation)
- **Neutrals**: Clean whites and dark foregrounds
- **High Contrast**: Ensures readability and impact

**Typography**:
- **Font Family**: Plus Jakarta Sans (premium, modern, accessible)
- **Weights**: 300-800 for hierarchy and emphasis
- **Style**: Bold, uppercase for impact; clean body text for readability

**Design Elements**:
- Thick borders (4px standard)
- Hard shadows (Brutalist style: 4px-8px offsets)
- High contrast combinations
- Bold, uppercase headings
- Interactive hover effects (shadow removal + translation)

### Mobile-First Design
**TikTok-Style Experience**:
- Full-screen vertical scrolling (100vh sections)
- Snap-scroll navigation
- Video-centric content
- Touch-optimized interactions (48px minimum targets)
- Immersive storytelling approach

This approach targets modern parents who consume content on mobile and expect social media-quality experiences.
